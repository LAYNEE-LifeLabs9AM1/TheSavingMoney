For whom is this website?

This website is for people who are willing to learn on how to save money and for the people who have difficulty in saving money. This website is also willing to help other participants who are just here to listen to some tips that will be presented in the website in order for them to understand the importance of saving money. If we could, we may also invite some entrepreneurs and experts to also give their pieces of advice regarding the idea at the website. This idea may help them to save money, and a few tips on how to stop the urge of spending too much money, instead, investing it to more particular things that grow more expensive in the future such as investments.
